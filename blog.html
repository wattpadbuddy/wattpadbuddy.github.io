Wattpad


Wattpad is the most widely used smartphone ebook reader. In November 2006, Wattpad was created by Allen Lau and Ivan Yuen in a garage with the vision of "changing the way we share stories." The company is based on Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The concept of the company was that readers could download an app and read and discuss fiction written by professional and aspiring authors from all over the world. Wattpad surpassed one million users in 2011. Its app has been downloaded five million times and supports 1,000 phone models from 600 different mobile carriers.


“Wattpadders in the Philippines have embraced the future of entertainment, where technology and storytelling come together to empower new voices,” added by Mr. Lau. “Filipino readers, writers, and entertainment executives were first to understand the true scope of our vision to entertain and connect the world through stories. As a result, hundreds of Filipino Wattpad stories have been turned into books, TV shows, and films, changing how entertainment gets made in the country.”

Due to the success it's success, Wattpad became popular here in the Philippines. The country became the second largest consumer of Wattpad with over 7 million users. In 2014, Wattpad has signed a deal with Summit Media's Pop Fiction imprint to print Filipino Wattpad stories in the Philippines.
Some of the stories made by Filipino authors have turned into movies.

Why does Wattpad so popular?


Wattpad is so popular all over the world. It's feature becomes engaging with the users. You can be able to write stories on your own. The writers or authors are free to create and write their own masterpiece without spending big amount. They get to write stories that makes the audience relates it to their real life scenario. Most of the time, authors found themselves writing the things inside their heart instead of saying it. The readers can read all the stories they want. With Wattpad, the users are allowed to vote, comment, and share the stories. They can be able to connect and interact with the different authors. All you need is to create an account so you can start reading. Just add your chosen story to the library, then you can already read as much as you want. It can ease your boredom away.
