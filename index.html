What is Watppad?

Wattpad is a website and app that allows authors to share new user-generated stories with the world. It aims to bring together amateur and professional writers to form social communities around their stories.

Learn more
Why does Wattpad so popular?

Wattpad is a platform that allows the readers to write and read stories. It is a platform where users can discover their talent in writing stories, and readers can enjoy stories and find a new hobby to enjoy with their free time.

What are the genres available in Wattpad?

A lot of stories in Wattpad are classified based on their genres. The readers can choose what type of stories they would like to read. Over 21 genres are available, from Teen Fiction to General Fiction and from Romance to Horror stories.
