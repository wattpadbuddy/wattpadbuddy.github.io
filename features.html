GENRES

ROMANCE

A romance is a type of story that follows the development of a relationship between two people as they try to resolve their differences or the events of their past.

Learn More >
TEEN FICTION

Teen fiction depicts the ups and downs of adolescence through the mental, physical, and social interactions of a teen or young adult protagonist with whom readers can relate.

Learn More >
GENERAL FICTION

General fiction, also known as contemporary fiction, focuses on a characters daily life and conflicts. General Fiction is written for adults and contains more mature themes.

Learn More >
FANTASY

Fantasy is a form of fiction that takes place in a different universe - an imagined realm populated by mystical and supernatural powers.

Learn More >
FANFICTION

Fan fiction is a form of writing that uses existing characters, settings, and plots to tell new, original stories. In certain cases, actual people are used as characters in fan fiction tales.

Learn More >
SCIENCE FICTION

Science fiction is usually set in a futuristic or space age universe in which imaginative and technical advances are possible while adhering to the story's existing natural laws.

Learn More >
SHORT STORY

A short story is a piece of narrative prose that focuses on a single, pivotal plot event. Short stories are significantly shorter and more compact that gives the reader a glimpse or slice of life.

Learn More >
HUMOR

Humor stories seek to entertain and amuse with fun, fancy or ridiculousness, while simultaneously constructing a narrative that resonates with the reader.

Learn More >
HISTORICAL FICTION

Historical fiction often tells the story of a famous historical event or a memorable, familiar time period as seen through the eyes of fictional characters.

Learn More >
VAMPIRE & WEREWOLF

Vampire literature is mainly concerned with vampires who prey on living beings in order to survive. Werewolf fiction often focuses on supernatural transformation of a human into a wolf - and its repercussions

Learn More >
ACTION & ADVENTURE

Adventure fiction is characterized by action, threat, and suspense, and it focuses on a specific challenge, quest, or journey that the protagonist or hero must complete successfully.

Learn More >
MYSTERY & THRILLER

Mystery story investigates a crime, a challenge, a puzzle, or some other unknown. Thriller stories focuses on uncovering mysteries, they move at a faster pace and are driven by action, tension, excitement, and violence. 

Learn More >

Most Followed and Famous Authors

Jonah Mae Panen Palanca

@jonaxx

Jonaxx is know as the "Pop Fiction Queen" and the "Best Selling Author" She is the first Filipino Wattpad author who reached 2 M. followers in Wattpad and got verified. Today, she has already reach 4.84 M. followers on Wattpad. Jonaxx is known for her mind-blowing and jaw dropping plot twist in her every story. Her first published book is "Mapapansin Kaya"

Maria Cristina Lata

@blue_maiden

Tina Lata is an author who inspires a lot of people (Wattpaders) to believe in fairy tales and second chances. She already has 3 million followers on her Wattpad account. Her most popular story is "The Four Bad Boys and Me" with over 277 million reads.  

Maxine Lat Calibuso

@maxinejiji

Maxine Lat Calibuso is a Filipino author best known for the Wattpad series "He's Into Her." with 239 million reads. Her works were published under Life is Beautiful (LIB Creatives)/Pastrybug, a division of Precious Pages Corporation. She currently has 3.39 million followers in Wattpad.

Abigail De Silva

@CeCelib

Cecelib is a Waray author and is known for writing mature content stories or R-18. Her famous works is "Possessive Series" with 22 series of that story and the 23rd book is still on-going. She currently has 62 published stories and has 3.19 million followers on Wattpad.

Alyssa Mae Almario

Ariesa Jane Domingo

Denny R.
